Voyages was designed to provide teachers with many different options. Not only will you find three main types of activities (Preflight Training, Launch and Expeditions) but you can find the same content given different emphasis in different activities.

 

For example, one activity may focus on students discovering through observation the nature of the brightness magnitude scale while another activity might require Preflight Training in magnitudes.   In the latter case, magnitude is presented as a tool to explore a different concept.  Another activity might assume a higher level of prior knowledge about magnitude and explore origins of these measurements at the image processing and data analysis levels.

 

This section helps you to plan your trip through Voyages. Your starting place is the answer to the question:  What do you need from Voyages?

I need Voyages to provide:

 • supplemental content-specific or standard specific activities and resources to fit into my established curriculum

•  a pathway to astronomy content over an extended time frame

•  enrichment experiences for small groups of students or individuals

•  content pathway to explore information technology and engineering

•  research experiences for individuals or groups
